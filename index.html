About We're Just Buds

Where Great prices and Fantastic Customer Service meet! We are a small but VERY friendly recreational cannabis store in Pullman Washington home of the WSU Cougars (GO COUGS!). We take pride in being one of the smallest but also on of the best stores on the Palouse.
